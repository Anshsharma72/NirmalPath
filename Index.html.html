About Us

Nirmal Path, meaning "Clean Path," is a movement aimed at revolutionizing waste management. We believe that technology and community effort can create a sustainable and clean environment. Our platform leverages AI and a unique QR-based system to encourage responsible waste disposal and reward eco-conscious behavior.

We are a team of passionate innovators, environmentalists, and community builders dedicated to making a tangible difference. Our goal is to empower individuals to take small, actionable steps that collectively lead to a cleaner, greener planet for future generations.
